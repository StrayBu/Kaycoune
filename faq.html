Frequently Asked Questions

Do you treat people's sicknesses?

As Kaycoune Success Empowerment Initiative (SEI), we focus on providing medical outreaches, treating minor ailments, and offering health education to prevent and manage medical conditions. Our services include: Medical Outreaches: - Free health screenings - Medical consultations - Dispensing of medications - Referrals to specialized care Minor Ailments: - Treatment of common illnesses like malaria, typhoid, and diarrhea - Management of minor injuries - Provision of first aid Health Education: - Workshops on disease prevention - Training on healthy habits - Awareness campaigns on prevalent health issues Partnerships: - Collaborations with healthcare providers for specialized care - Partnerships with local organizations for community outreach Target Population: - Underserved communities - Rural areas - Low-income households Our Goal: - Improve access to quality healthcare - Reduce morbidity and mortality rates - Empower individuals with health knowledge Note: Kaycoune SEI is a non-profit organization, and our services are tailored to support vulnerable populations. We do not provide comprehensive medical treatment for severe conditions. Instead, we focus on preventive care, minor ailments, and referrals to specialized care when needed.

Do you make hospital referral?

At kaycoune Success Empowerment Initiative, we make referrals to hospitals for continuity of care.
